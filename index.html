ABOUT D.O.A Limited

We provide responsive high quality and cost effective services

D.O.A Limited is an independent and wholly (100%) Indigenous Engineering Company. Dedicated to providing responsive high quality and cost effective services to clients who are mainly in the construction field. Roads, Buildings and Telecoms base stations site builds.

D.O.A Ltd was formed and currently been controlled by group of Engineers and Architects who are adepts in their own various professions, having practiced continuously for numerous years in their various fields. D.O.A Ltd was established in Nigeria and registered by the corporate affairs Commission. It is a front-line privately-owned engineering and architectural company formed to carryout projects as contained in the Memorandums of Article.
